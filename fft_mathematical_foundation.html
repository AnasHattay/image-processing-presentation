FFT on Signals

Continuous 2D Fourier Transform
For continuous signals f(x,y)
F(u,v) = ∫-∞∞∫-∞∞ f(x,y) e-j2π(ux+vy) dx dy
Forward transform: Space → Frequency
f(x,y) = ∫-∞∞∫-∞∞ F(u,v) ej2π(ux+vy) du dv
Inverse transform: Frequency → Space
f(x,y) Input signal in spatial domain
F(u,v) Output in frequency domain
u,v Spatial frequency coordinates
x,y Spatial coordinates
Discrete 2D Fourier Transform
For digital images f[m,n]
F[k,l] = ∑m=0N-1∑n=0N-1 f[m,n] e-j2π(km+ln)/N
Forward transform: Discrete space → Frequency
f[m,n] = 1/N² ∑k=0N-1∑l=0N-1 F[k,l] ej2π(km+ln)/N
Inverse transform: Frequency → Discrete space
f[m,n] Discrete input signal (N×N samples)
F[k,l] Discrete frequency domain
k,l Frequency bin indices (0 to N-1)
m,n Sample indices (0 to N-1)
