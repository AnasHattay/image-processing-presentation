Mathematical Foundation

F(u,v) = Σ Σ f(x,y) × e^(-j2π(ux+vy)/N)
Where: F(u,v) = frequency domain, f(x,y) = spatial domain, N = image size

Key Insight: Images can be represented as a sum of sinusoidal waves of different frequencies and orientations.
